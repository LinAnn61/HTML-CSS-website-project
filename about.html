About KodeChunx

KodeChunx is a website resource designed with the absolute novice to coding skills in mind. Changes in the UK labour market in 2023 and some years previously, have seen a rise in opportunities in the digital sector. Also, the State Retirement Age has increased and more senior people will be expected to work for longer, before being eligible for their pension. Some Jobseekers of all ages may be seeking career changes from first principles for myriad reasons and granular introductions to new employment opportunities are of pivotal importance.



It may be the case, that someone is interested in a career in the digital sector, but is not familiar with any of the “tech speak”, or computer usage other than emails and online shopping. In some cases, prior digital knowledge may be zero. Simple introductory level glossaries are needed, progressing to the fundamentals of coding, before established accredited courses of Level 1 or above can be undertaken. The journey to a new digital career from absolute scratch has to start somewhere. That start is KodeChunx.
